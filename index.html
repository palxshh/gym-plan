HOW THIS PLAN WORKS

⚡
Upper / Lower split — each muscle hit twice a week. The proven sweet spot for beginners: enough to grow, not enough to burn out. Always start every session on the Warm-Up tab.
📈
Progressive overload is the whole game. Each week beat last week — one more rep, or +2.5 kg. That single habit drives 90% of your results.
⏱️
Rest between sets. 60–90 sec for small muscles (arms, calves, abs); 90–120 sec for big lifts (squat, press, row). Use the timer in the bottom corner.
🏠
Your home base counts. Squats, mountain climbers & skipping already gave you leg strength, a cardio engine and coordination — you're ahead of most beginners.

HOW TO READ EACH EXERCISE

3 × 10–12
3 sets of 10 to 12 reps. Rest, then repeat.
Tempo 2-1-2
Seconds: 2 down · 1 pause · 2 up. Slow = safer & more muscle.
🫁 Breathing
Breathe OUT on the hard part (the lift), IN on the way back.
🏋️ Start weight
A suggested first weight for an average beginner — adjust it to you.
Pick weight
Right weight = last 2 reps are hard but form holds. Too easy? +2.5 kg next time.
🔁 Swap
If a machine is taken, use the listed alternative — don't wait around.

⚠️ FORM FIRST — THE BEGINNER RULES

1️⃣
For your first 1–2 weeks, go lighter than these suggestions. Learn the movement first — strength comes fast once form is locked.
2️⃣
On big lifts, do 1–2 warm-up sets with a lighter weight before your working sets.
3️⃣
Sharp pain = stop. Muscle burn = good. Never trade form for heavier weight.
5 MIN

WARM-UP FIRST

Never skip this. Warming up wakes up your muscles, lubricates joints, and slashes injury risk. Do this before every session.

🟢
Step 1 — 2 min light cardio. Brisk treadmill walk, cycle, or your skipping rope. Just enough to feel slightly warm.
🟡
Step 2 — the 3 mobility moves below. 10–12 reps each, slow and controlled.
🔵
Step 3 — warm-up sets. On your first big lift, do 1–2 sets with light weight before going to your working weight.
Arm Circles
10 forward · 10 backward
Big slow circles · loosen the shoulders
Shoulders
  • Keep arms straight, draw the biggest circle you can
  • Switch direction halfway
✓ You know this!
Bodyweight Squats
12 reps · slow
Sit down & up · no weight · full depth
Legs + Hips
  • Exactly your home squat — use it to grease the movement
  • Go as deep as feels comfortable
Leg Swings
10 each leg
Swing one leg front-to-back · hold something for balance
Hips + Hamstrings
  • Relaxed, controlled swings — don't force the range
  • Then switch to the other leg
MON · DAY 1

PUSH

Chest, Shoulders, Triceps — the pushing muscles. Warm up first!

Dumbbell Chest Press
3 × 10–12 · Tempo 2-1-2
01
Lower to chest → press straight up
Chest🏋️ 10 kg each
🫁 Exhale as you press up · inhale lowering
  • Feet flat on floor, slight natural arch in lower back
  • Lower to chest level, press up & slightly inward
⚠️ Avoid: bouncing the dumbbells off your chest or flaring elbows straight out to 90°.
🔁 Swap: Chest Press Machine or flat barbell bench.
Shoulder Press
3 × 10–12 · Tempo 2-0-2
02
From shoulders → press overhead
Shoulders🏋️ 8 kg each
🫁 Exhale pressing up · inhale lowering to ears
  • Start at ear level, elbows ~90°
  • Brace your core — don't lean back
⚠️ Avoid: arching your lower back to heave the weight up — the #1 beginner mistake.
🔁 Swap: Shoulder Press Machine.
Cable Chest Fly
3 × 12–15 · Tempo 3-1-1
03
Arms wide → squeeze together in front
Inner Chest🏋️ 8 kg each side
🫁 Exhale bringing hands together · inhale opening
  • Keep a soft, fixed bend in the elbows the whole time
  • "Hug a big tree" — squeeze chest at the front
⚠️ Avoid: bending & straightening the elbows (that turns it into a press, not a fly).
🔁 Swap: Pec-Deck (machine fly) or dumbbell flyes on a bench.
Tricep Pushdown
3 × 12–15 · Tempo 2-1-2
04
Elbows pinned · push bar down till arms straight
Triceps🏋️ 15 kg stack
🫁 Exhale pushing down · inhale on the way up
  • Elbows glued to your sides — only the forearms move
  • Fully straighten arms, squeeze at the bottom
⚠️ Avoid: leaning over the bar and using bodyweight to push it down.
🔁 Swap: Overhead dumbbell tricep extension or bench dips.
Plank
3 × 30–45 sec hold
05
Body in one straight line — hold steady
Core💪 Bodyweight
🫁 Breathe normally — never hold your breath
  • Same setup as mountain climbers — on forearms here
  • Squeeze glutes + abs, neck neutral (look at floor)
⚠️ Avoid: letting hips sag down or pike up — keep the straight line.
TUE · DAY 2

LOWER A

Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves — your biggest muscles burn the most fat.

✓ You know this!
Goblet Squat
3 × 10–12 · Tempo 2-1-2
01
Hold DB at chest · sit down to parallel · stand up
Quads + Glutes🏋️ 16 kg DB
🫁 Inhale going down · exhale driving up
  • Just your home squat holding a dumbbell at chest height
  • Feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out, chest tall
⚠️ Avoid: knees caving inward or heels lifting off the floor.
🔁 Swap: Leg Press (already in this day) or Smith-machine squat.
Leg Press
3 × 12–15 · Tempo 2-0-2
02
Bend knees to ~90° → push the plate away
Quads + Glutes🏋️ 60 kg
🫁 Exhale pushing away · inhale bending in
  • Feet shoulder-width, mid-height on the plate
  • Lower until knees ~90°, keep lower back on the seat
⚠️ Avoid: locking knees hard at the top, or letting hips curl off the seat at the bottom.
🔁 Swap: Goblet squats or Hack-squat machine.
Romanian Deadlift
3 × 10–12 · Tempo 3-0-2
03
Push hips BACK · lower weights down shins · stand up
Hamstrings + Glutes🏋️ 12 kg each
🫁 Inhale hinging down · exhale standing up
  • Think "push hips back to a wall behind you," not "bend down"
  • Keep back flat, slight knee bend, weights close to legs
⚠️ Avoid: rounding your back — biggest injury risk here. Stop at the stretch, not the floor.
🔁 Swap: Seated leg-curl machine or back-extension.
Leg Curl (Machine)
3 × 12–15 · Tempo 2-1-3
04
Lie face down · curl heels toward your glutes
Hamstrings🏋️ 25 kg stack
🫁 Exhale curling up · inhale lowering slow
  • Ankles under the pad, hips pressed into the bench
  • Squeeze at the top, lower over 3 slow seconds
⚠️ Avoid: letting your hips pop up off the pad to swing the weight.
🔁 Swap: Seated leg curl or dumbbell RDLs.
Calf Raises
3 × 15–20 · Tempo 2-1-2
05
Rise onto tiptoes · pause · lower for a stretch
Calves💪 BW → +10 kg
🫁 Exhale rising · inhale lowering
  • Use a step so heels can drop below for full range
  • Pause 1 sec at the top — calves love high reps
⚠️ Avoid: fast tiny bounces — slow and full range builds calves.
🔁 Swap: Seated or standing calf-raise machine.
THU · DAY 4

PULL

Back, Biceps, Rear Shoulders — the pulling muscles that balance your pushing.

Lat Pulldown
3 × 10–12 · Tempo 2-1-2
01
Pull the bar down to your upper chest
Back + Biceps🏋️ 35 kg stack
🫁 Exhale pulling down · inhale letting it rise
  • Grip a bit wider than shoulders, lean back slightly
  • Drive elbows down to your ribs — lead with elbows
⚠️ Avoid: yanking the bar behind your neck or leaning way back to cheat.
🔁 Swap: Assisted pull-up machine or band pulldowns.
Seated Cable Row
3 × 10–12 · Tempo 2-1-2
02
Reach forward → pull handle to your belly
Mid-Back + Biceps🏋️ 35 kg stack
🫁 Exhale pulling in · inhale reaching out
  • Sit tall — don't round your back reaching forward
  • Pull to belly button, squeeze shoulder blades together
⚠️ Avoid: rowing with your lower back by rocking back & forth.
🔁 Swap: Chest-supported row machine or dumbbell rows.
Dumbbell Bicep Curl
3 × 12–15 · Tempo 2-1-2
03
Elbow pinned · curl weight up to shoulder
Biceps🏋️ 8 kg each
🫁 Exhale curling up · inhale lowering
  • Elbows pinned to your sides — they don't drift forward
  • Lower slowly, fully straighten arm at the bottom
⚠️ Avoid: swinging your body to fling the weight up. No swing = right weight.
🔁 Swap: Cable curls or barbell curls.
Face Pulls
3 × 15 · Tempo 2-1-2
04
Pull rope to your face · elbows HIGH & wide
Rear Shoulders🏋️ 10 kg stack
🫁 Exhale pulling to face · inhale extending out
  • Rope at face height, elbows higher than your hands
  • Imagine doing a double biceps pose at the end
⚠️ Avoid: going too heavy — this is a light "posture & shoulder-health" move.
🔁 Swap: Reverse pec-deck or band pull-aparts.
Lateral Raises
3 × 12–15 · Tempo 2-1-2
05
Raise arms out to the sides like wings
Side Shoulders🏋️ 5 kg each
🫁 Exhale raising · inhale lowering
  • Tiny bend in the elbows, lead with your elbows
  • Raise only to shoulder height — no higher
⚠️ Avoid: going heavy and shrugging — light weight, strict form here.
🔁 Swap: Lateral-raise machine or cable side raises.
FRI · DAY 5

LOWER B + CORE

Second lower session — more glute & quad focus, finished with core.

Leg Press
3 × 12–15 · Tempo 2-0-2
01
Feet a touch higher today for more glute
Quads + Glutes🏋️ 60–70 kg
🫁 Exhale pushing away · inhale bending in
  • Place feet slightly higher on the plate to bias glutes
  • You should be a bit stronger than Tuesday — nudge it up
⚠️ Avoid: half-reps — control the full range down to ~90°.
🔁 Swap: Goblet squats or hack-squat.
Walking Lunges
3 × 10 each leg
02
Step forward · drop back knee toward floor · alternate
Quads + Glutes🏋️ 8 kg each / BW
🫁 Inhale stepping down · exhale driving up
  • Step far enough that the front knee stays over the ankle
  • Start bodyweight — balance is the real challenge
⚠️ Avoid: short steps that push the front knee way past the toes.
🔁 Swap: Static split squats holding a rack for balance.
Leg Extension
3 × 12–15 · Tempo 2-1-3
03
Extend legs straight out · squeeze quads
Quads🏋️ 30 kg stack
🫁 Exhale extending · inhale lowering slow
  • Back against the pad, shin pad on your ankles
  • Straighten fully, squeeze hard, lower over 3 sec
⚠️ Avoid: swinging the weight up with momentum or slamming it down.
🔁 Swap: Goblet squats or Sissy squats.
Hip Thrust
3 × 12 · Tempo 2-1-2
04
Drive hips UP to a flat bridge · squeeze glutes
Glutes💪 BW → +10 kg
🫁 Exhale driving hips up · inhale lowering
  • Upper back on the bench, feet flat, knees ~90° at top
  • Drive through your heels, squeeze glutes hard at the top
⚠️ Avoid: over-arching your lower back at the top — ribs down, squeeze glutes instead.
🔁 Swap: Glute-bridge on floor or hip-thrust machine.
Crunches + Leg Raises
3 × 15 each
05
Crunch: curl shoulders up · then 15 leg raises
Core / Abs💪 Bodyweight
🫁 Exhale crunching up · inhale lowering
  • Crunch = curl shoulders off floor, not a full sit-up
  • Leg raises: legs straight, lower slowly without touching down
⚠️ Avoid: yanking your head with your hands — hands rest, abs do the work.

THE RECOMP MINDSET

⚡
No crash diets. Eat at maintenance or a small deficit (~200–300 kcal). Let training do the work.
💧
2.5–3L water daily. Especially on gym days — dehydration kills performance.
😴
Sleep 7–8 hours. Muscle is built during sleep, not in the gym. Non-negotiable.
📅
Consistency > perfection. 12 weeks at 80% beats 3 weeks at 100% then quitting.
